Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering

Still have a question? We're just a call or WhatsApp away — happy to help you plan the perfect moment.

📞 88821 58973
Are all your cakes eggless?+
Yes — absolutely! Every single cake at HFC is 100% eggless. We believe celebrations should be inclusive, so we never use eggs in any of our recipes. This has always been and will always remain our promise.
Do you deliver outside Faridabad?+
Yes! We now deliver across Faridabad and the wider Delhi NCR region. Please call or WhatsApp us with your location to confirm delivery availability and timing for your area.
How far in advance should I place my order?+
For cakes, we recommend at least 24–48 hours in advance. For wedding and event decorations, please contact us at least 5–7 days before the event. For flower bunches, same-day orders are often possible.
Can I customise my cake design?+
Absolutely! Customisation is our specialty. Share your idea, theme, colors, or reference photo over WhatsApp and we'll create something uniquely yours. Photo prints, character themes, floral designs — we do it all.
What flowers do you use for decoration?+
We use fresh, seasonal flowers sourced daily — roses, marigolds, lilies, orchids, gerberas, carnations and more. All arrangements are made fresh on the day of your event to ensure maximum fragrance and beauty.
How do I place an order?+
The easiest way is to WhatsApp or call us directly. For cakes, call 8882158973. For flowers and decorations, call 9899595618. You can also visit us at Shop No. 12, Omaxe Hills 2, Sector 43, Faridabad.
